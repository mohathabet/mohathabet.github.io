TIn this project, I had the privilege of capturing a heartwarming event at Marriott Hotel Sousse, dedicated to honoring the hardworking hotel staff for their exceptional dedication and efforts throughout the season. Through the lens of my camera, I documented the special moments of gratitude, appreciation, and celebration. From heartfelt speeches to touching gestures, the video captured the genuine appreciation extended to the workers. The final video aimed to showcase the hotel's commitment to recognizing and valuing their dedicated team, highlighting the spirit of unity and excellence that defines Marriott Hotel Sousse.
