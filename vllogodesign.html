VL logo design

Description
I had the privilege of designing a captivating logo for the Village of Languages, a renowned language school in Mahdia. The objective was to create a logo that represents the school's commitment to language education, cultural diversity, and transformative learning.
Services
BrandingWebflow
Development
Client
Village de langue
Mahdia

Objective

The primary objective of this project was to create a logo that represents the essence of the Village of Languages. The logo needed to convey the school's commitment to language education, cultural diversity, and the transformative power of learning.

‍

Design Process

To begin, I conducted in-depth research on the Village of Languages, immersing myself in its vision, values, and unique approach to language instruction. This research provided valuable insights into the school's atmosphere and inspired the design direction.

Next, I brainstormed and sketched multiple concepts, seeking to capture the essence of language and cultural exchange in a visually compelling way. I explored various symbols, typography styles, and color schemes to create a design that resonates with students, educators, and language enthusiasts.

After gathering feedback and refining the concepts, I transitioned to the digital realm, utilizing design software to bring the logo to life. I paid careful attention to typography, selecting a font that balances professionalism and approachability. The color palette was chosen to reflect the diverse languages taught at the school, while the visual elements incorporated symbols representing communication and connection.

‍

Result

The final logo embodies the school's spirit, attracting students from diverse backgrounds and becoming an integral part of the Village of Languages' visual identity. It symbolizes unity, inspiration, and the transformative nature of language learning, establishing the school as a prominent institution in Mahdia.
