As the first-place winner of my university's logo design competition for its 20th-anniversary celebration, My visually striking logo symbolizes excellence, advancement, and innovation. It's an honor to leave a lasting mark on this significant milestone in our university's history.

ISIM Monastir

Objective

The primary objective of the logo design project was to create a visually captivating and symbolic logo that effectively commemorates the 20th anniversary of the computer science and mathematics university in Monastir. The logo needed to encompass the institution's values, achievements, and its strong connection to the field while embodying a sense of excellence, innovation, and growth.

Design Process

The design process commenced with thorough research, delving into the university's rich history, core values, and its unique position in Monastir. The iconic Ribat monument tower, a significant landmark in the city, emerged as a compelling source of inspiration. Elements such as leaves, symbolizing achievement and growth, a book representing knowledge and education, a star denoting excellence, and a web Earth signifying the digital realm were carefully chosen to reflect the university's key attributes.

Through multiple iterations and sketches, different compositions and arrangements were explored, aiming to harmoniously incorporate the selected elements while considering visual impact, balance, and symbolism. Feedback from stakeholders, including the university administration and target audience, played a crucial role in refining and fine-tuning the design.

Result

The final logo design is a remarkable representation of the university's identity and its 20-year milestone celebration. The tower-inspired by the Ribat monument stands tall, symbolizing strength, heritage, and the university's significance in Monastir. The inclusion of leaves serves as a metaphor for continuous growth, progress, and academic achievement. The book signifies the institution's commitment to knowledge and education, while the star represents its pursuit of excellence and recognition. The web Earth denotes the university's focus on cutting-edge technology and the digital landscape.

By incorporating the number "20" into the logo, it effectively captures the significance of the anniversary celebration. The chosen colors, typography, and overall design aesthetics further enhance the logo's visual appeal and versatility across various applications.
